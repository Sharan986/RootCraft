Jharkhand's Heritage, Crafting
Traditions with Pride
Rootcraft is dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage of Jharkhand. We offer a range of
authentic handcrafted products, including tribal art and traditional crafts, while empowering local artisans.

Recommended Products

In Our Gallery

Jharkhand heritage products feature intricate woodwork,
vibrant Sohrai and Khovar paintings, tribal ornaments, stone carvings,
and traditional dolls, reflecting the state's rich cultural and artistic legacy.

Our Arts

Jharkhand's arts showcase tribal heritage through vibrant colors,
nature-inspired designs, Dokra metalwork, Sohrai paintings, and sustainable craftsmanship.
